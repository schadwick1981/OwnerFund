Statement of Ownership and Funding
Construction on Private Property

To the Property Owner and Authorized Representative:

Before construction begins, Parkway Construction asks each project owner to confirm ownership of the property and the availability of funds sufficient to pay for the work. This is a standard, routine step in commercial construction that protects everyone involved: it confirms financing is in place before work starts, reduces the risk of mid‑project payment disruptions or work stoppages, and supports a smooth, predictable draw and payment process.

This statement is provided to comply with U.S. Code Section 3903 and applicable state construction‑funding statutes. Completing it does not create any obligation beyond the terms of your construction contract; it simply verifies, in writing, the funding that will support the project.

Please complete Items 1 through 8, arrange the applicable Lender, Bank, and Owner certifications, and return the notarized form to us before construction begins. We are glad to answer any questions as you complete it, and we appreciate your partnership.

How to complete this form
  • Complete every applicable field in Items 1–8. If an item does not apply, check “Not applicable.”
  • Item 6 (construction loan) is certified and notarized by an authorized officer of the Lender.
  • Item 7 (funds on deposit) is certified and notarized by an authorized officer of the Bank.
  • Item 8 must reconcile: total funds available must equal or exceed the contract amount, including a change‑order reserve.
  • The Owner / Primary Obligor certification must be signed and notarized. Return the completed form before construction begins.
